About Me


I am a final-year B. Tech student at Indian Institute of Information technology, Una. I am a dedicated and innovative web developer, continuously learning new design trends to create interactive and responsive designs. As a web designer, my objective is to make a positive impact on clients, co-workers, and the Internet using my skills and experience to design compelling and attractive websites. Currently, I am honing my skills working with Figma, a powerful web-based design tool. I am well-versed in C++ and DSA too.
I'm also an imaginative content writer and love creating compelling articles and short stories. I'm continuously searching for new topics and stories to capture the attention of new readers. With my knowledge and experience, I can help you fulfill your content creation goals.
Apart from this, I write poems. One of my poems got published in two anthologies, From My Heart and Tranquil Volume-1. I love to sing. I have a youtube channel where I post my song covers.
Moreover, I am a bookworm. I like reading all genres, but fiction, especially Horror, has all my heart. My favourite fictional book so far is ‘To Kill A Mockingbird’, by Harper Lee. Other hobbies include drawing, playing badminton, and watching movies/web series.
